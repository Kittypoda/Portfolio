Oslo Science Museum

Oslo Science Museum, or OSM, is a modern science museum for children aged 7 to 15. The science museum has a strong focus on future technology, environmental awareness, and learning.

Whether it's building a robot, experimenting with renewable energy sources, or exploring the mysteries of space, Oslo Science Museum provides a dynamic and inspiring setting for young explorers to discover the wonders of science.

Project brief

Project brief Design and build a modern, accessible and responsive web presence for the client using the provided image and text content.

The site should appeal to youngsters without pandering; while aiming at a younger audience it should not patronise them with overtly childish themes. The core purpose of the site is to excite and entice people to visit the museum.

The client has not supplied a logo because the museum committee cannot decide on a final design. You are free to create a placeholder logo for the client
